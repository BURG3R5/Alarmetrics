<p>Alarmetrics listens to device notifications and tracks snoozed alarms, reminders, and calendar events.</p><p><strong>Features:</strong><ul><li>Tracking: Automatically detects and records snoozed alarms, reminders, and events</li><li>Visualization: Graphs your snooze history across four time scales.</li><li>Private: Fully offline; your data never leaves your device.</li><li>Lightweight: Runs in the background with minimal battery and memory usage.</li></ul></p>
